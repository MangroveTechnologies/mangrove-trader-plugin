Look up the user's trading stats on MangroveTrader.

## Steps

1. Ask for their Twitter handle if not known
2. Call `trader_my_stats` with `twitter_handle`
3. Present: Composite Score, Rank, Total Trades, Qualified status, Open Positions
4. Suggest `/mangrove-trader:status` for full tool list or the performance report for detailed breakdown
